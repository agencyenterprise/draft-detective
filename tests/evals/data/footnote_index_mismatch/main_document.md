# Site Assessment Report for AI Data Center Development

## Executive Summary

This report evaluates potential sites for AI data center development across the United States. The assessment considers power availability, grid connectivity, infrastructure readiness, and regulatory environment.

## Task 1: Framework Development

We developed a multicriteria framework to systematically assess site suitability for large-scale AI infrastructure deployment. The framework incorporates technical, economic, and regulatory factors relevant to data center operations.

## Task 2: Site Assessment

Using this framework, we assessed 22 sites: 17 identified by DOE, two private-sector developments by the Stargate consortium, and three recently retired or retiring power plants.[[10]](#footnote-10)[[11]](#footnote-11)

The assessment revealed significant variation in site readiness and power availability across regions.

## Task 3: Capacity Analysis

Our analysis indicates that substantial new generation capacity will be required to meet projected AI workload demands by 2030.
